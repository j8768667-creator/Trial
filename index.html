Chord Arranger Lite

Create synchronised, editable MIDI arrangements across nine genres, with custom song structure and in-browser preview.
Separate chords with commas, spaces or vertical bars. Add (N) right after a chord to hold it for N bars, e.g. Am(2) F(1) C(1) G(4) — default is 1 bar. If the progression is shorter than the song, it loops. Supports major, minor, 6, m6, 7, maj7, m7, maj9, 9, m9, sus2, sus4, add9, dim, dim7, m7b5, aug and slash basses (e.g. "C/E").
Comma-separated Name:bars sections, e.g. Verse:8. Recognized names (Intro, Verse, Pre-Chorus, Chorus, Bridge, Breakdown, Solo, Outro) shape each section's energy and drum/bass behaviour; any other custom label falls back to medium energy. Total song length = sum of all sections.
